Cheque Payments additional information

Please do not post date the cheques.* Can cause the clubs to pay fees if the cheque is rejected.

Organisers get a lot of cheques and its not always clear whos cheque is for who so on the back of the cheque for each paddlers fees write:
£*
(name, division, class, M/W, Bib, Single/Double, *Fee Amount)

Remember to sign the cheque

*Exception: If you are entering a competition that is 6mths or more away, then post-date the cheque to five months before the competition date

Email Entries

If you are entering a Prem or Div 1 event then DO NOT send an email entry. It will be ignored! Only possible exception is if you are entering to do an official run.

For other divisions use online entry system if one is provided. If you want to enter by email, please remember that:

  • If the event fills up then those who have sent payment will take priority
  • If your entry is accepted then the rules state that you are still required to pay, EVEN IF YOU CHANGE YOUR MIND. However, at div 3/4 events organisers will be more lienient so just tell them in advance if it is a provisional entry. They would probably rather have the provisional entries.
  • It is better not to both enter by email and by post as this just means that the organiser has to cross check
